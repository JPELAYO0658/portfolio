Bar

Tequila, the star of Viva’s show! Our selection of tequilas and mezcal was curated to highlight what makes tequila such a special spirit; it’s distinctive flavors. From the citrus notes in blancos, to vanilla in reposados, and the woody complexity in añejos, tequila has so much more to offer. Viva’s bar program takes advantage of tequila’s natural pairing with fresh fruits. Our craft cocktails are made with fresh fruits and syrups and shrubs made in-house. Our expert bartenders shake and stir up a cocktail that will leave you thirsty for more.

Kitchen

Our kitchen produces a full menu for lunch & dinner service. Carrying a foundation of Guadalajara-based Mexican cuisine, Viva’s menu ranges from traditional fare such as tortas to specialties like our Oaxacan mole. While our standout dishes include grilled carnes and house-made chorizo links, we still have the ever-popular street tacos. Viva takes pride in using fresh, seasonal ingredients in its dishes.

About Us

Viva opened in 2015 in the neighborhood of Dupont, WA. With an aesthetic that combines the space’s warm Mexican ceramics, Viva is where you can unwind with a craft margarita and tender carnitas. Our bar is outfitted with multiple tv screens, so that everyone in the house can view the big game! Our dedicated team of servers, hosts, and bartenders provide hospitality that is friendly and inviting. Given the large space Viva can comfortably accommodate multiple parties of eight, with a section that can host up to 20!
